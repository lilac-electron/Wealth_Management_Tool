Welcome to Fins-Fintech!

What do we do?

We provide a range of finance tools aimed at making financial awareness as easy as opening your favorite browser!

Some of the services we provide include:

  • Portfolio Projection
  • Finance Calculators
  • Outgoings Management
  • Asset Management
  • Transaction tracking

Who are we?

We are a final year project student studying computer science at the University of Sussex

This project is designed to be a wealth management tool in python calculating net worth using the simple formula: Net worth = Assets - Credits

This tool aims to enhance financial literacy, in a way that is normally hidden behind paywalls or limited to people who can afford accountants.
